Car Troubleshooting Guide

Engine Issues

If your engine is not starting, check the following:

  • Ensure the battery is charged.
  • Check for fuel.
  • Inspect the ignition system.
  • Always get your car engine air filter replaced after one year of use.
  • An aged spark plug creates a weak ignition and can stop an engine from turning over i.e. misfiring.
  • Overheating usually happens due to coolant loss that can be a result of a bad radiator, thermostat, hose, water pump or a faulty cooling fan.

Transmission Problems

If you're experiencing transmission issues, try these steps:

  • Check the transmission fluid level.
  • Inspect for any leaks.
  • Ensure proper fluid color and consistency.
  • Never change gears while driving.
  • Change your transmission filter.
  • Flush your transmission.

Car Electrical Repair

If there is any electrical repair, check the following:

  • Identify the problem.
  • Test the translator.
  • Check the fuses.
  • Inspect the wiring.
  • Check the device that connects to the home grid or the wiring and tries disconnecting the power boards or devices from the outlet.
  • If you hear a buzzing sound emanating from them, you should have them replaced without delay.
